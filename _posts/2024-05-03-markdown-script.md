## Lists

### Ordered list

2. 2nd item
3. 3rd item

### Unordered list

- Chapter

### ToDo list

- [ ] Job
  + [x] Step 1
  + [x] Step 2
  + [ ] Step 3

### Description list

Sun
: the star around which the earth orbits

Moon
: the natural satellite of the earth, visible by reflected light from the sun
